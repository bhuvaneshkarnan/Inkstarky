I've been in the industry for over 5 years, establishing Inkstarky Tattoo in February 2021. Originally from Baguio, I've brought a meticulous, detail-oriented approach to black and grey realism right here to Dagupan City.

My work is highly technical. Whether it's a horror portrait, a sleeve, or an intricate piece of fine art on skin, the goal is always precision and longevity. I don't just put ink in skin; I craft a piece that heals perfectly and holds its depth.

Aftercare

Proper healing preserves the fine details of realism work. Follow these strict guidelines.

Download PDF Guide

Keep it clean

Wash gently with unscented antibacterial soap 2-3 times a day.

Hydrate sparingly

Apply a paper-thin layer of approved aftercare ointment. Do not over-moisturize.

No sun, no soaking

Avoid direct sunlight, swimming pools, and baths for at least two weeks.
